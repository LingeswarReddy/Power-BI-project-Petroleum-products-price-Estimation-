# Petroleum Product Price Analysis Dashboard – README

## Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes the **price trends of Superior Kerosene Oil under the Non-Public Distribution System (Non-PDS)** across major cities in India. It provides a visual overview of price distribution by **year, city, and time period**, helping users understand how kerosene prices vary geographically and historically.

The dashboard is designed to support **data analysis, comparison, and quick insights** through interactive visualizations and filters.

---

## Key Objectives

* Analyze kerosene oil prices across different cities in India.
* Identify yearly price trends.
* Compare price distribution among cities.
* Allow users to filter data by **month, country, city, and date**.
* Provide quick summary metrics through KPI cards.

---

## Dashboard Features

### 1. KPI Summary Cards

The top section provides quick insights:

* **First Country:** Displays the selected country (India).
* **Count of City Name:** Shows the total number of city records available (940).
* **Last City Name:** Displays the last city in the dataset (Mumbai).
* **%GT Count of City Name:** Shows the percentage contribution of selected cities (100%).

These cards provide a **quick overview of dataset statistics**.

---

### 2. City-wise Comparison Chart

This horizontal bar chart shows the **count of records by city**.

Cities included:

* Chennai
* Delhi
* Kolkata
* Mumbai

Each city displays its **percentage contribution**, allowing easy comparison of data coverage across cities.

---

### 3. Year-wise Price Trend

The yearly bar chart shows the **total price of Superior Kerosene Oil (Non-PDS)** aggregated by year.

Key observations:

* Prices show fluctuations over different years.
* Certain years show higher cumulative price values indicating possible price increases or higher recorded transactions.

Values are represented in **INR (Indian Rupees)** with scaling applied for readability.

---

### 4. City-wise Price Distribution

The donut chart represents the **distribution of total kerosene prices across cities**.

Each segment represents:

* Mumbai
* Chennai
* Kolkata
* Delhi

The chart helps identify which city contributes the **largest share of total kerosene price values**.

---

## Interactive Filters (Slicers)

Users can interact with the dashboard using the following filters:

### Month Filter

Allows selection of specific months such as:

* April
* August
* December

### Country Filter

Currently includes:

* India

### City Filter

Users can select specific cities:

* Chennai
* Delhi
* Kolkata
* Mumbai

### Date Filter

Allows filtering data by specific dates.

### Clear All Slicers

A button is provided to **reset all filters instantly**.
